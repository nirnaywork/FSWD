Crispy Chicken

Description Of Crispy Chicken

Chicken Biryani is a fragrant and flavorful South Asian rice dish made with aromatic basmati rice, tender chicken, and a blend of rich spices. The chicken is first marinated in yogurt and spices, then cooked with caramelized onions, tomatoes, and herbs to create a deep, savory masala. Layers of partially cooked basmati rice are added on top, along with saffron milk, fried onions, and fresh herbs. The dish is then slow-cooked (dum style) to let the flavors blend beautifully. Ingredients

  • 500 g chicken (boneless or small pieces)
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 1 tbsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cornflour
  • Oil for deep frying

Steps to prepare Chicken Biriyani!

  1. Marinate chicken in buttermilk, chili powder, garlic, pepper, and salt for 1 hour.
  2. Mix all-purpose flour and cornflour with a pinch of salt and chili powder.
  3. Coat marinated chicken in the flour mix, pressing gently to make it stick.
  4. Deep fry in hot oil until golden and crisp.
  5. Drain excess oil and serve with mayonnaise or spicy sauce.
